You retain moral authorship of your Contribution. The Foundation shall not remove or obscure your attribution from files or records to which you have contributed.

Upon acceptance of your first Contribution into the production codebase, you become entitled to participate in the Foundation's quarterly revenue distribution process as described in the OFA Foundation Charter (Section 5).

a) Your existing revenue share entitlement for quarters in which you participated is unaffected.

b) Future revenue share entitlement is governed by the OFA Foundation Charter (Section 9), including provisions on inactivity and voluntary departure.

c) Your Contribution remains part of the Licensed Work and continues to be licensed under the OFA License. Your license grant under Section 2 of this Agreement is irrevocable.

d) Your moral rights and attribution under Section 3 are unaffected.
